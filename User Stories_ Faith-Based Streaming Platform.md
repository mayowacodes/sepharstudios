# **User Stories: Faith-Based Streaming Platform**

## **1\. Faith-Based Entertainment Access**

### **Title: As a family-friendly viewer, I want to access faith-based entertainment so that I can enjoy inspirational content with my family.**

### **User Type:**

* General audience (families, individuals seeking faith-based content)

### **Acceptance Criteria:**

#### **Landing Page Experience**

* When I visit the homepage, I should see a clear and inviting message about faith-based entertainment.  
* I should be able to start a free trial or learn more about the service through visible CTA buttons.

#### **Content Accessibility**

* I should be able to browse an extensive library of faith-based movies, shows, and documentaries.  
* I should be able to create up to **8 family profiles** for personalized recommendations.  
* Content should be available in **high quality, up to 4K HDR**.

#### **Cross-Device Viewing**

* I should be able to stream content on various devices (phone, tablet, laptop, TV).  
* I should be able to **switch between profiles seamlessly**.  
* I should have the ability to **download content for offline viewing**.

#### **User Navigation & Support**

* I should be able to easily find support options like **Help Center, Device Support, and Contact Information**.  
* I should be able to access legal information such as **Terms of Service and Privacy Policy**.

#### **Social Media & Engagement**

* I should be able to connect with the platform via social media links (**Facebook, Twitter, Instagram**).

## **2\. Enhanced User Story Additions**

### **Personalization & User Engagement**

* As a user, I want to **receive content recommendations** based on my viewing history and preferences.  
* As a parent, I want **parental controls** to restrict certain content for kids.  
* As a frequent viewer, I want a **“Continue Watching” section** to resume my shows from where I left off.

### **Subscription & Payment Experience**

* As a potential subscriber, I want to **view different subscription plans** before signing up.  
* As a user, I want to **receive a free trial** before committing to a paid plan.  
* As a paying subscriber, I want **secure and flexible payment options** (credit card, PayPal, etc.).  
* As a user, I want to **manage my subscription** (upgrade, downgrade, cancel) easily through my account settings.  
* As a **premium user**, I want **family plan options** with **exclusive parental controls**.

### **Freemium, Premium & Pay-Per-View Models**

#### **Freemium User**

* As a **freemium user**, I want to explore **limited content for free** to decide if the platform is worth subscribing to.  
* As a **freemium user**, I want **non-intrusive ads and upgrade prompts**.  
* As a **returning user**, I want to **retain my progress and preferences**.

#### **Premium User**

* As a **premium subscriber**, I want **unlimited access to exclusive content**.  
* As a **paying member**, I expect **priority support and an ad-free experience**.

#### **Pay-Per-View (PPV) User**

* As a **casual viewer**, I want to **pay only for specific content**.  
* As a **PPV customer**, I expect **clear pricing and refund policies**.

### **Kids Section Enhancements**

* As a parent, I want **age-specific content** curated for different age groups (**Kids & Teenagers**).  
* As a child or teen, I want to access **interactive Bible stories, quizzes, or educational videos**.  
* As a parent, I want a **safe exploration mode** that prevents access to adult content.  
* As a parent, I want **viewing reports** to track my child's activity.  
* As a user, I want **playlists, watchlists, watch history, and "Continue Watching" sections** for both adult and kids sections.

### **Content Discovery & Personalization**

* As a user, I want **AI-driven recommendations** based on my watch history and preferences.  
* As a user, I want to create and share **customizable playlists**.  
* As a user, I want content categorized by **faith-based themes** (e.g., Hope, Family, Prayer).

### **Community & Engagement Features**

* As a viewer, I want to **leave reviews and ratings** for movies and shows.  
* As a community member, I want to **participate in faith-based discussions or forums**.  
* As a user, I want to **receive email updates or notifications** about new content releases.  
* As a social user, I want to **host group watch parties** with family and friends remotely.

### **User Retention & Performance**

* As a user, I want **fast-loading and buffer-free streaming**.  
* As a mobile user, I want an **optimized experience with a dedicated mobile app**.  
* As a user, I want my **data and personal information to be securely stored and protected**.  
* As a user with slow internet, I want **adaptive streaming** for uninterrupted playback.  
* As a loyal subscriber, I want **loyalty rewards** like discounts, badges, or free months.  
* As an engaged user, I want **gamification elements** like streaks, achievements, and points.  
* As a long-term user, I want **personal milestones** to celebrate anniversaries or watch milestones.

## **4\. Color Integration & Branding**

* **Freemium Highlights & CTA Buttons:** `#FF5E0E` (Vibrant Orange) – Creates urgency for upgrades & engagement.  
* **Premium Exclusive Features:** `#FFBF00` (Gold) – Represents luxury and exclusivity.  
* **Backgrounds, Borders, & Depth:** `#AF6E4D` (Earthy Brown) – Adds contrast and warmth.  
* **Background Themes:**  
  * **Off-White Mode** for a clean and bright experience.  
  * **Dark Mode** for a comfortable low-light viewing experience.
