12. Tabernacle/Temple Builder

Build the Tabernacle or Solomon's Temple based on biblical descriptions
Learn about symbolism and meaning
Rewards: Accuracy in construction, completion bonuses
Tokens: "Builder Blocks" for architectural achievements

Game 1: Apostles' Table (Poker-Style)
Core Concept
Texas Hold'em poker reimagined with biblical themes and Christian values. Instead of traditional poker hands, players form "faith combinations" based on Christian virtues and biblical stories.
Theme Integration
Card Suits (Traditional → Christian)

♠️ Spades → ⚔️ Swords of the Spirit (Spiritual warfare, strength)
♥️ Hearts → ❤️ Hearts of Love (Love, compassion, grace)
♦️ Diamonds → ✝️ Crosses (Sacrifice, redemption)
♣️ Clubs → 🕊️ Doves (Peace, Holy Spirit, wisdom)

Face Cards

Kings: Biblical kings (David, Solomon, Hezekiah, Josiah)
Queens: Biblical women (Esther, Ruth, Mary, Deborah)
Jacks: Prophets/Disciples (Peter, Paul, John, Isaiah)

Hand Rankings (Christian-Themed)

Divine Hand (Royal Flush) - Strongest hand, represents God's perfect plan
Twelve Disciples (Straight Flush) - Unity and completeness
Trinity Power (Four of a Kind) - Father, Son, Holy Spirit symbolism
Faith House (Full House) - Built on solid foundation
Grace Flow (Flush) - Continuous blessing
Righteous Path (Straight) - Walking the straight and narrow
Unity Witness (Three of a Kind) - Agreement in testimony
Two by Two (Two Pair) - Noah's Ark, disciples sent out
Foundation Pair (One Pair) - Two witnesses, partnership
Single Stone (High Card) - David's stone against Goliath

Gameplay Mechanics
Tournament Structure

Disciples' Circle: 12-player tournaments (like the 12 apostles)
Small Group Tables: 4-6 player cash games
Sunday Championship: Weekly mega tournament

Token System

Faith Tokens: Primary currency earned through play
Grace Chips: Bonus tokens for good sportsmanship
Offering Tokens: Optional charity contributions from winnings

Special Features

Prayer Break: 60-second timer between tournament rounds with inspirational quotes
Encouragement Chat: Positive messages only, moderated
Fold with Grace: Leave tables politely with automated kind messages
Tithing Option: Donate 10% of winnings to featured ministries

Educational Elements

Each card displays a Bible verse when clicked
Hand names link to relevant biblical stories
Loading screens show biblical wisdom about wisdom, patience, strategy
